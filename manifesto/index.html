Core Convictions

Constraint as generative force. The most powerful work emerges from what it refuses to do. I believe in using limitation — of materials, marks, colors, elements — not as restriction but as the engine that drives everything else. This period proved constraint can manifest through pure formal destruction as much as material reduction.
